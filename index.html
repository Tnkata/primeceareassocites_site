About Us

Prime Care Associates Services is a private home health care agency whose mission is to assist clients in their homes ensuring safety, comfort, independence and dignity. We provide 24 hour care services 7 days a week and cover a wide range of services including, nursing, rehabilitative, hospice care, and assistive care.
